who is pasta jesus ?

Pasta Jesus is a graffitittag that Amalie noticed on Nørrebro,
and decided to name her t-shirt enterprise after.
PASTA JESUS believes in the egocentric soul, and promotes
self-love through wearing ones own damn face.
All designs and items are hand crafted by Amalie in her humble
home in Copenhagen, Denmark. What a girl.
